Services for every dog and cat

Boarding

Your pets stay overnight in your sitter’s home. They’ll be

treated like part of the family in a comfortable environment.

House Sitting

Your sitter takes care of your pets and your house. Your pets

will get all the attention they need from the comfort of home.

Dog Walking

Your dog gets a walk around your neighborhood. Perfect for

busy days and dogs with extra energy to burn.

Doggy Day Care

Your dog spends the day at your sitter’s home. Drop them off

in the morning and pick up a happy pup in the evening.

Drop-In Visits

Your sitter drops by your home to play with your pets, offer

food, and give potty breaks or clean the litter box.

Dog Training

Private, virtual dog training through PURRFECT. Train from the

comfort of your home through 1-1 video chat.
